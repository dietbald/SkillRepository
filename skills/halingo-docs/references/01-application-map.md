# Halingo Application Map & Functional Groups

This document provides a comprehensive overview of all available screens and the core functional groupings within the Halingo SaaS platform.

## 1. Available Screens (Site Map)

### **A. Core Navigation & Dashboard**
- **Main Dashboard**: Real-time overview of practice metrics and daily schedule.
- **Practice Selector**: Dropdown to switch between different practice locations or clinics.
- **User Profile Settings**: Manage personal details, email, and password.

### **B. Agenda & Scheduling**
- **Main Calendar**: Day, Week, and Month views for session management.
- **Appointment Modal**: Form to create/edit sessions, link patients, and set nomenclature.
- **Private Appointment Screen**: For scheduling non-patient related blocks.
- **Rosa Integration Dashboard**: Configuration for external booking synchronization.

### **C. Patient Management (Patiëntendossiers)**
- **Patient List**: Searchable overview of all patients with status tags (e.g., Waitlist).
- **Patient Detail Overview**: General contact and demographic information.
- **Reimbursement Tab (Terugbetaling)**: Management of insurance plans, session counts, and RIZIV rules.
- **Documents & Reports**: Digital storage for clinical reports, PDF uploads, and session notes.
- **Session History**: Chronological log of all past and upcoming treatments for a specific patient.

### **D. Financial & Billing (Financieel)**
- **Invoice Overview**: List of all generated invoices with payment status.
- **Batch Invoicing (Verzamelstaten)**: Grouping invoices for health insurance funds (Ziekenfondsen).
- **Session Billing Screen**: Interface to select unbilled sessions and generate new invoices.
- **Certificate Print Modal (Getuigschrift)**: Specialized screen for matrix printer alignment and RIZIV certificate generation.
- **Commission Overview**: Financial breakdown for group practices with multiple therapists.

### **E. Settings & Practice Management**
- **General Practice Settings**: Configure logo, practice details, and global invoice comments.
- **Email Template Editor**: Customize automated patient communications.
- **Subscription Management**: Manage Halingo SaaS plan, payment methods, and billing history.
- **Nomenclature/Tariff Settings**: Configure rates according to "De Conventie" or private tariffs.

## 2. Functional Grouping (20 Core Competencies)

1.  **Identity Management**: User authentication, profile security, and role management.
2.  **Practice Branding**: Customizing the look and feel of outgoing documents (Invoices/Emails).
3.  **Patient Data Privacy**: GDPR-compliant storage of sensitive medical and personal data.
4.  **Waitlist Optimization**: Managing and prioritizing new patient inquiries.
5.  **Multi-View Scheduling**: Flexible calendar management for individual or group practices.
6.  **Treatment Planning**: Mapping treatments to Belgian RIZIV nomenclature codes.
7.  **Reimbursement Tracking**: Automated counting of sessions against approved insurance limits.
8.  **Compliance Monitoring**: Ensuring billing aligns with the latest Belgian healthcare conventions ("De Conventie").
9.  **Clinical Reporting**: Tools for creating, storing, and extending treatment reports.
10. **Document Digitization**: Uploading and linking external PDFs/images to patient files.
11. **Smart Invoicing**: One-click generation of invoices from completed sessions.
12. **Payment Lifecycle Management**: Tracking "Open," "Paid," and "Overdue" statuses.
13. **Debt Collection**: Sending automated or manual payment reminders via email.
14. **Mutualistic Billing**: Streamlined generation of "Verzamelstaten" for insurance funds.
15. **Precision Printing**: Advanced alignment tools for matrix printers and official RIZIV forms.
16. **Patient Communication**: Automated email notifications for appointments and invoices.
17. **Telehealth Integration**: Built-in support for video consultations.
18. **External Platform Sync**: Real-time synchronization with booking platforms like Rosa.be.
19. **Practice Analytics**: High-level financial and operational performance reporting.
20. **SaaS Lifecycle**: Integrated subscription management and billing for the Halingo platform itself.
